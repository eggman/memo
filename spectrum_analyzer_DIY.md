# DIY spectrum analyzer


# Chips

## A7105
* 2.4GHz
* 128 channels
* 8 bit RSSI

## A7130
* 2.4GHz
* 128 channels
* 8bit RSSI

## ATR2406
* 2.4GHz
* 95 channels
* RSSI analog out (0.1 - 1.9 V)

## CC2500
* 2.4GHz
* 8 bit RSSI


## CYRF6936
* 2.4GHz
* 1MHz channel
* 5bit RSSI (update every 12us)

## nRF24L01
* 2.4GHz
* 127 RF channels 
* no RSSI
* 1bit RPD (Received Power Detector)
