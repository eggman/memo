# DIY spectrum analyzer

# Chips

## A7105
* 2.4GHz
* 128 channels
* 8 bit RSSI

## A7130
* 2.4GHz
* 128 channels
* 8bit RSSI

## AR928X
* 2.4GHz, 5,8GHz
* OFDM subcarrier (56 carreies in 20MHz mode, 114 carries in 40MHz mode)
* 8bit magnitude (|i|+|q|)
* https://wiki.freebsd.org/dev/ath_hal%284%29/SpectralScan

## ATR2406
* 2.4GHz
* 95 channels
* RSSI analog out (0.1 - 1.9 V)

## ATR2820
* 5.8 GHz
* 142 chnnels
* RSSI analog out 

## CC2500
* 2.4GHz
* 8 bit RSSI

## CYRF6936
* 2.4GHz
* 1MHz channel
* 5bit RSSI (update every 12us)

## ML5800
* 5.8GHz
* RSSI analog out

## ML5805
* 5.8GHz
* RSSI analog out

## nRF24L01
* 2.4GHz
* 127 RF channels 
* no RSSI
* 1bit RPD (Received Power Detector)
